Instructions & Information

  • Solution color changes based on pH: red (acidic), purple (neutral), blue (basic)
  • Strong Acid + Strong Base: Equivalence point at pH 7
  • Weak Acid + Strong Base: Equivalence point at pH > 7 (basic)
  • Strong Acid + Weak Base: Equivalence point at pH < 7 (acidic)
